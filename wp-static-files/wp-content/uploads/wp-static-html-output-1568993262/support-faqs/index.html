SUPPORT &
FAQS

What is Gifting?
Gifting is a unique feature on the HipBar app that you can use to gift drinks to anybody. On HipBar, you can gift SKU gift cards (gifting a specific product) or cash gift cards (gifting a certain cash amt.) On receiving a gift card, the recipient can head to the store & pick up the drink gifted (SKU gift card) or a product of their choice with the amount gifted (HipBar Cash Value card).
What are HipBar gift cards? What can I gift with them?
There are 2 types of gift cards you can choose from. You can gift either:
  • A product (SKU based gift card) from the HipBar catalogue
  • A HipBar Cash Value card with a value determined by the sender of the gift card
What can I buy with a product gift card?
If you’ve received an Product (SKU) gift card, you will be able to redeem that specific drink that’s gifted to you. If you’ve received a Cash Value card, you will be able to redeem any drink for the relevant value of the gift card.
How do I gift a product gift card?
Add to the cart the drink you wish to gift from any retail outlet Select “Gift or request a friend to pick up” Enter the recipient’s information Review the gift card information and pay That’s it! Please note that the recipient will be notified of the gift and can redeem the gift card at their convenience.
What is my gift card validity?
One year from the date the sender sent you the gift card. For example, if the sender sent you the gift card on 04th July 2019, it’ll be valid till 03rd Oct 2020. If the gift card has expired, please contact Customer Support.
